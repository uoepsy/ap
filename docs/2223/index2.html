Applied Psychometrics
2022/23

Course description

The course is a survey of topics related to psychological measurement as used in various real-world settings, psychometric theory and models, test construction, and the interpretation of results and test manuals. The course emphasizes the practical side of these topics, allowing those who complete the course to both construct some psychological assessment scales, present and interpret their key psychometric properties, and evaluate these properties when provided for other scales.
